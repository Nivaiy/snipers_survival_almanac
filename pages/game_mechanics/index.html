Crafting.

Crafting is the beginning of progress in the game; without it, you cannot obtain useful resources for survival. All you need are a maximum of 2 different items and their required quantities. First, you need to select an item (by clicking on it in your hand) and press the button labeled 'craft,' then choose an additional item in the other hand. After that, press the button again, and if you have combined the items correctly, the crafted item will appear in the hand you previously selected.

Also, in the game, you can craft not only in your hands but also on workbenches, in kettles, and in the furnace.

Technology research.

The Technology Menu is the most important part of progression in the game. It allows you to unlock new crafts and abilities. All you need for research is experience. Experience can be obtained from blocks, crafting, workbenches, brewing in a kettle, and from smelted items in the furnace.

With new research or when changing locations, new technologies for research will be unlocked. So don’t forget to keep progressing, because the further you go, the more content will become available.

Resource Gathering.

In the game, this is done quite simply. You need a specific tool that can break a certain type of block, and the middle mouse button. Activate the hand slot with the tool and press the middle mouse button for the first time. Then select a block and press the middle mouse button again. After that, when the outline becomes solid (not transparent), you will be able to mine the block by swinging the tool.

But for some tools, you don’t need to swing in both directions, such as the axe. With it, you only need to swing in the direction of its sharp edge.
